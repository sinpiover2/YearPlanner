# Version 1 Scope

> "Version 1 is complete when Year Planner becomes Jeff's trusted daily teaching workspace."

---

# Purpose

This document defines the boundaries of Version 1.

It answers three questions:

1. What absolutely must exist before school begins?
2. What intentionally waits until after Version 1?
3. How do we prevent scope creep while preserving the long-term vision?

This document is intended to protect the project from building exciting features before solving the teacher's daily workflow.

---

# The Mission

Version 1 is not an AI product.

Version 1 is not a reflection product.

Version 1 is not a teacher development platform.

Version 1 is a trustworthy daily teaching workspace.

Nothing more.

Nothing less.

---

# Definition of Success

Version 1 succeeds when Jeff can honestly say:

> "I no longer need FileMaker to teach."

It does **not** require that every future idea be implemented.

It requires that the daily workflow be complete, reliable, and trustworthy.

---

# Version 1 Includes

## Forecast

The teacher always knows:

- Am I OK?
- Am I on pace?
- Where do I have flexibility?
- What deserves attention?

Forecast exists to support professional judgment.

Forecast does not replace professional judgment.

---

## Planning

Planning should support:

- Weekly planning
- Session scheduling
- Unit awareness
- Forecast awareness
- Daily preparation

Planning should feel calm.

Planning should reduce anxiety.

---

## Lesson Session

Lesson Session becomes the teacher's thinking workspace.

It supports:

- Lesson authoring
- Teaching Episodes
- Deliverables
- Materials
- Printing

Planning happens here.

Thinking happens here.

Revision happens here.

---

## Printing

Printing is a first-class feature.

The printed lesson is the live teaching document.

Printing represents the transition from thinking to teaching.

# Architectural Requirements

Although many future capabilities are deferred, Version 1 should not prevent them.

Architecture should remain compatible with:

- Reflection
- Voice input
- AI reconstruction
- Pattern detection
- Teacher coaching
- Long-term curriculum evolution

The architecture prepares for these ideas without implementing them.

---

# Scope Discipline

Every proposed feature must answer:

## Does this help Jeff teach before August 1?

If no:

It belongs after Version 1.

---

## Does this reduce the chance Jeff opens FileMaker?

If no:

It belongs after Version 1.

---

## Does this improve one step in the Daily Cognitive Workflow?

If no:

It belongs after Version 1.

# Version 1 Principles

## Calm before capability.

Reducing anxiety has higher priority than adding features.

---

## Simplicity before automation.

The workflow should become obvious before becoming intelligent.

---

## Trust before AI.

AI cannot compensate for an untrustworthy workspace.

---

## Teacher judgment remains central.

The application supplies context.

The teacher makes decisions.

---

## Good planning creates freedom.

Planning exists to make better instructional decisions possible.

It never forces instructional decisions.

---

## Teaching is live.

The software prepares the teacher.

The teacher teaches students.

The software returns afterward.

---

# What We Are Building

We are not building software that teaches.

We are not building software that replaces teachers.

We are building software that protects the teacher's limited cognitive energy so that more of it reaches students.

Everything in Version 1 should contribute directly to that purpose.

---

# Exit Criteria

Version 1 is complete when all of the following are true:

- Weekly planning is performed in Year Planner.
- Daily lesson preparation is performed in Year Planner.
- Lessons are printed from Year Planner.
- Teaching relies on the printed lesson and clipboard.
- Navigation feels natural.
- Forecast is trusted.
- FileMaker is no longer needed.

When those conditions are satisfied:

Stop adding Version 1 features.

Teach with the application.

# Final Decision Filter

Whenever uncertainty exists, ask one question:

> **Does building this today make it more likely that Jeff will leave FileMaker behind on the first day of school?**

If yes:

Build it.

If no:

Record it.

Protect it.

Build it after Version 1.
